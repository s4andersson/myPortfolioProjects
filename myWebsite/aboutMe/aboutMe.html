Who Am I

I’m a Swedish man born in 1992 and raised in a small town in the forests of Värmland. My passions include physical training, gaming, and programming—fields that challenge me to think creatively and solve problems.

My career has taken me on a unique journey. I started as a welder and steel construction worker, traveling as an entrepreneur for hire. Seeking a change, I transitioned to personal training and massage, and became certified in the field. However, I soon realized it wasn’t the right fit for me.

Today, I’m pursuing a Master of Science in Computer Engineering at the University of Karlstad. Programming has become a true passion, combining my love for problem-solving and continuous learning. I’m excited about the future and the opportunities this field brings.
